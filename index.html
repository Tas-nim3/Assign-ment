Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Fostering creativity and embracing new technologies to drive economic growth and societal progress.

Sustainability

Promoting eco-friendly practices and renewable energy solutions to protect the environment for future generations.

Infrastructure

Developing modern infrastructure to enhance connectivity, transportation, and economic growth.

Empowerment

Enabling individuals and communities through education, skill development, and inclusive opportunities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and friendly future. Green energy powers a sustainable future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Let us strive to build a world where compassion and innovation go hand in hand, ensuring that no one is left behind in our journey toward a fair and just society. When we create opportunities for one, we create progress for all."



Dr. Muhammad Yunus


Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
